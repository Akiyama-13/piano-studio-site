About the Instructor
Pat Yu is a dedicated piano instructor with extensive experience in performance and music education. Trained in classical piano from a young age, she later continued her Bachelor's Program at California State University Northridge, where she strengthened her skills in piano performance, music theory, and pedagogical methods.

Before teaching full-time, she was actively involved in solo performances and collaborative piano work. She also taught group lessons and private classes for undergraduate students as a Graduate Teaching Assistant at Wichita State University. These experiences shaped her clear, structured, and patient teaching style.

She has worked with students ranging from young beginners to adult learners. Her lessons are highly personalized— whether a student aims to build foundational skills or simply enjoy playing music, she designs a supportive and achievable learning plan.
